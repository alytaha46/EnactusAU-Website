Who are we

Enactus is an international nonprofit organization dedicated to inspiring students to improve the world through entrepreneurial action. We provide a platform for teams of outstanding university students to create community development projects that put the people's own ingenuity and talents at the center of improving their livelihoods. Guided by educators and supported by business leaders, our students take kind of entrpreneurial approach that empowers people to be part of their own success. Our work transforms both the lives of the people we serve, and in turn, the lives of our students as they develop into more eãective, value - driven leaders.
